About

The Club focuses on extracting the hidden talent of the students
in the field of Information Technology
To improve and enhance the technical skill set of the students by collaborating the club with leading Software Industries
To mould students for industry ready expertise by organizing various technical activities

Activities

CSE Club has conducted a workshop on "Python for Data Sciences instead of Machine learning" on 12th March,2020.

Mr.Nirmal Saravanan, SOFTWARE DEVELOPER from HEXAWARE has trained the students on the theme of the workshop.

Computer Science Club has conducted "Ethical Hacking Workshop" for Second and Third year students of CSE on 7th February, 2019 in Digital Library.

Mr.Raja Perumal, CEO of Integrated Resource Management Solutions (IRMS), Chennai and his team of resource persons trained the students.

CSE club has conducted Linux course for our students on 4th August,2018

Hardware Club

Hardware club has conducted IoT workshop on 4th and 5th September, 2018.

Dr. Alber Mayan and Mr.Damodaran of CSE department has trained the student participants.

Kidzilla is an event initiated by Sathyabama Firefox Club and adopted by the Mozilla Community throughout the world.

Basically the event is all about teaching the government school children about web technologies.

WoMoZ -(Women in Mozilla) is a wing of Mozilla club to empower the women in Computer Science Club.

Talentmeet 2018, a 5 day technical event was organized by the young minds of Computer Science Club of SDC in our campus from 23rd to 27th January 2018.

About 800 students participated and benefited out of this event which hosted 40+ technical sessions.
